Frequently Asked Questions

Why buy life insurance? +

There are essentially two reasons people buy life insurance: they owe someone or they love someone.

Life insurance helps ensure your financial responsibilities are met if something happens to you. It can:

  • Cover funeral costs, mortgages, and other outstanding debts
  • Help your loved ones maintain their current standard of living
  • Support future goals such as college tuition or charitable giving
When should I buy life insurance? +

The sooner, the better. It's never too early to prepare for the unexpected. When you're young and healthy, your risk is lowerâ€”so premiums are typically much more affordable.

As you go through major life events like getting married, having children, or buying a home, it's wise to review and adjust your coverage to match your evolving needs.

What kind of life insurance should I buy? +

There are essentially two types of life insuranceâ€”term and permanent.

Term Insurance generally offers coverage for a temporary period (e.g., 10 or 20 years) at lower costs, often with an option to convert to permanent coverage later.

Permanent Insurance typically provides lifetime coverage with a fixed premium and builds cash value over time.

How much life insurance should I own? +

Most experts agree that owning seven to ten times your annual salary in life insurance is a good rule of thumb. This amount helps ensure your beneficiary can become financially re-established after your passing.

Why buy an annuity? +

An annuity can help you reach your retirement goals by offering tax-deferred growth and the opportunity to receive a guaranteed stream of income. It provides security against market downturns and fluctuating interest rates.
